11.41 I have in the past addressed You as \xc3\x92O K\xe2\x80\x9e\xc5\x92\xe2\x80\x9aa,\xc3\x93 \xc3\x92O Y\xe2\x88\x92dava,\xc3\x93 \xc3\x92O my friend,\xc3\x93 without knowing Your glories.
