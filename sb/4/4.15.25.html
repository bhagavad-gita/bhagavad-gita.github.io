25 prabhavo hy atmanah stotram jugupsanty api visrutah hrimantah paramodarah paurusam va vigarhitam SYNONYMS prabhavah--very powerful persons; hi--certainly; atmanah--of themselves; stotram--praise; jugupsanti-- do not like; api--although; visrutah--very famous; hri-mantah--modest; parama-udarah--very magnanimous persons; paurusam--powerful actions; va--also; vigarhitam--abominable. TRANSLATION As a person with a sense of honor and magnanimity does not like to hear about his abominable actions, a person who is very famous and powerful does not like to hear himself praised.
