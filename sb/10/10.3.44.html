44 sandisya sadhu-lokasya kadane kadana-priyan kama-rupa-dharan diksu danavan grham avisat SYNONYMS sandisya--after giving permission; sadhu-lokasya--of the saintly persons; kadane--in persecution; kadana-priyan-- to the demons, who were very expert at persecuting others; kama-rupa-dharan--who could assume any form, according to their own desire; diksu--in all directions; danavan--to the demons; grham avisat--Kamsa entered his own palace. TRANSLATION These demons, the followers of Kamsa, were expert at persecuting others, especially the Vaisnavas, and could assume any form they desired. After giving these demons permission to go everywhere and persecute the saintly persons, Kamsa entered his palace.
