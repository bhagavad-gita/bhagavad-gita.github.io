10.6.16

Text

he ramagaccha tatasu sanujah kula-nandana pratar eva krtaharas tad bhavan bhoktum arhati

Synonyms

he rama--my dear son Balarama; agaccha--please come here; tata--my dear darling; asu--immediately; sa-anujah-- with Your younger brother; kula-nandana--the great hope of our family; pratah eva--certainly in the morning; krtaaharah-- have taken Your breakfast; tat--therefore; bhavan--You; bhoktum--to eat something more; arhati--deserve.

Translation

My dear Baladeva, best of our family, please come immediately with Your younger brother, Krsna. You both ate in the morning, and now You ought to eat something more.
