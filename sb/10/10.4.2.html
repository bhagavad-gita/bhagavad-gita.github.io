10.4.2

Text

tam drstva parama-pritah pratyutthaya krtanjalih anarcadhoksaja-dhiya pranipata-purahsaram

Synonyms

tam--him (Gargamuni); drstva--after seeing; parama-pritah--Nanda Maharaja was very much pleased; pratyutthaya--standing up to receive him; krta-anjalih--with folded hands; anarca--worshiped; adhoksaja-dhiya-- although Gargamuni was visible to the senses, Nanda Maharaja maintained a very high respect for him; pranipatapurahsaram-- Nanda Maharaja fell down before him and offered obeisances.

Translation

When Nanda Maharaja saw Gargamuni present at his home, Nanda was so pleased that he stood up to receive him with folded hands. Although seeing Gargamuni with his eyes, Nanda Maharaja could appreciate that Gargamuni was adhoksaja; that is, he was not an ordinary person seen by material senses.
