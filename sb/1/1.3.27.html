1.3.27

Text

rsayo manavo deva manu-putra mahaujasah kalah sarve harer eva saprajapatayah smrtah

Synonyms

rsayah--all the sages; manavah--all the Manus; devah--all the demigods; manu-putrah--all the descendants of Manu; maha-ojasah--very powerful; kalah--portion of the plenary portion; sarve--all collectively; hareh--of the Lord; eva--certainly; sa-prajapatayah--along with the Prajapatis; smrtah--are known.

Translation

All the rsis, Manus, demigods and descendants of Manu, who are especially powerful, are plenary portions or portions of the plenary portions of the Lord. This also includes the Prajapatis.

Purport

Those who are comparatively less powerful are called vibhuti, and those who are comparatively more powerful are called avesa incarnations.
