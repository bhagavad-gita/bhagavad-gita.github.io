3.17.3

Text

utpata bahavas tatra nipetur jayamanayoh divi bhuvy antarikse ca lokasyoru-bhayavahah

Synonyms

utpatah--natural disturbances; bahavah--many; tatra--there; nipetuh--occurred; jayamanayoh--on their birth; divi--in the heavenly planets; bhuvi--on the earth; antarikse--in outer space; ca--and; lokasya--to the world; uru--greatly; bhaya-avahah--causing fear.

Translation

On the birth of the two demons there were many natural disturbances, all very fearful and wonderful, in the heavenly planets, the earthly planets and in between them.
