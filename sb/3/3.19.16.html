16 punar gadam svam adaya bhramayantam abhiksnasah abhyadhavad dharih kruddhah samrambhad dasta-dacchadam SYNONYMS punah--again; gadam--mace; svam--his; adaya--having taken; bhramayantam--brandishing; abhiksnasah--repeatedly; abhyadhavat--rushed to meet; harih--the Personality of Godhead; kruddhah-- angry; samrambhat--in rage; dasta--bitten; dacchadam--his lip. TRANSLATION The Personality of Godhead now exhibited His anger and rushed to meet the demon, who bit his lip in rage, took up his mace again and began to repeatedly brandish it about.
