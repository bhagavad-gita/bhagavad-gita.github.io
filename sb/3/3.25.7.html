7 avadayams tada vyomni vaditrani ghanaghanah gayanti tam sma gandharva nrtyanty apsaraso muda SYNONYMS avadayan--sounded; tada--at that time; vyomni--in the sky; vaditrani--musical instruments; ghanaghanah--the rain clouds; gayanti--sang; tam--to Him; sma--certainly; gandharvah--the Gandharvas; nrtyanti--danced; apsarasah--the Apsaras; muda--in joyful ecstasy. TRANSLATION At the time of His descent on earth, demigods in the form of raining clouds sounded musical instruments in the sky. The celestial musicians, the Gandharvas, sang the glories of the Lord, while celestial dancing girls known as Apsaras danced in joyful ecstasy.
