3.23.20

Text

hamsa-paravata-vratais tatra tatra nikujitam krtriman manyamanaih svan adhiruhyadhiruhya ca

Synonyms

hamsa--of swans; paravata--of pigeons; vrataih--with multitudes; tatra tatra--here and there; nikujitam-- vibrated; krtriman--artificial; manyamanaih--thinking; svan--belonging to their own kind; adhiruhya adhiruhya--rising repeatedly; ca--and.

Translation

Here and there in that palace were multitudes of live swans and pigeons, as well as artificial swans and pigeons so lifelike that the real swans rose above them again and again, thinking them live birds like themselves. Thus the palace vibrated with the sounds of these birds.
