3.18.28

Text

distya tvam vihitam mrtyum ayam asaditah svayam vikramyainam mrdhe hatva lokan adhehi sarmani

Synonyms

distya--by fortune; tvam--to You; vihitam--ordained; mrtyum--death; ayam--this demon; asaditah--has come; svayam--of his own accord; vikramya--exhibiting Your prowess; enam--him; mrdhe--in the duel; hatva--killing; lokan--the worlds; adhehi--establish; sarmani--in peace.

Translation

This demon, luckily for us, has come of his own accord to You, his death ordained by You; therefore, exhibiting Your ways, kill him in the duel and establish the worlds in peace. Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports of the Third Canto, Eighteenth Chapter, of the Srimad-Bhagavatam, entitled "The Battle Between Lord Boar and the Demon Hiranyaksa."
