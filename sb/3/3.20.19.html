3.20.19

Text

visasarjatmanah kayam nabhinandams tamomayam jagrhur yaksa-raksamsi ratrim ksut-trt-samudbhavam

Synonyms

visasarja--threw off; atmanah--his own; kayam--body; na--not; abhinandan--being pleased; tamahmayam-- made of ignorance; jagrhuh--took possession; yaksa-raksamsi--the Yaksas and Raksasas; ratrim-- night; ksut--hunger; trt--thirst; samudbhavam--the source.

Translation

Out of disgust, Brahma threw off the body of ignorance, and taking this opportunity, Yaksas and Raksasas sprang for possession of the body, which continued to exist in the form of night. Night is the source of hunger and thirst.
