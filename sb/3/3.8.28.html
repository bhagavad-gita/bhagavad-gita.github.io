3.8.28

Text

kadamba-kinjalka-pisanga-vasasa svalankrtam mekhalaya nitambe harena cananta-dhanena vatsa srivatsa-vaksah-sthala-vallabhena

Synonyms

kadamba-kinjalka--saffron dust of the kadamba flower; pisanga--dress of the color; vasasa--by clothing; su-alankrtam--well decorated; mekhalaya--by the belt; nitambe--on the waist; harena--by the garland; ca-- also; ananta--highly; dhanena--valuable; vatsa--my dear Vidura; srivatsa--of the transcendental marking; vaksah-sthala--on the chest; vallabhena--very pleasing.

Translation

O my dear Vidura, the Lord's waist was covered with yellow cloth resembling the saffron dust of the kadamba flower, and it was encircled by a well-decorated belt. His chest was decorated with the srivatsa marking and a necklace of unlimited value.
