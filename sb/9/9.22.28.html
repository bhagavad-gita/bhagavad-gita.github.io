9.22.28

Text

jayadhvajat talajanghas tasya putra-satam tv abhut ksatram yat talajanghakhyam aurva-tejopasamhrtam

Synonyms

jayadhvajat--of Jayadhvaja; talajanghah--a son named Talajangha; tasya--of him (Talajangha); putrasatam-- one hundred sons; tu--indeed; abhut--were born; ksatram--a dynasty of ksatriyas; yat--which; talajangha-akhyam--were known as the Talajanghas; aurva-tejah--being very powerful; upasamhrtam-- were killed by Maharaja Sagara.

Translation

Jayadhvaja had a son named Talajangha, who had one hundred sons. All the ksatriyas in that dynasty, known as Talajangha, were annihilated by the great power received by Maharaja Sagara from Aurva Rsi.
