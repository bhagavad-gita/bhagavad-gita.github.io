9.7.16

Text

rohitas tad abhijnaya pituh karma cikirsitam prana-prepsur dhanus-panir aranyam pratyapadyata

Synonyms

rohitah--the son of Hariscandra; tat--this fact; abhijnaya--having thoroughly understood; pituh--of his father; karma--action; cikirsitam--which he was practically doing; prana-prepsuh--wishing to save his life; dhanuh-panih--taking his bow and arrows; aranyam--to the forest; pratyapadyata--left.

Translation

Rohita could understand that his father intended to offer him as the animal for sacrifice. Therefore, just to save himself from death, he equipped himself with bow and arrows and went to the forest.
