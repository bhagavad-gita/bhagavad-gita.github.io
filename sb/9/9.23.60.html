9.23.60

Text

karmany aparimeyani manasapi suresvaraih saha-sankarsanas cakre bhagavan madhusudanah

Synonyms

karmani--activities; aparimeyani--immeasurable, unlimited; manasa api--even by such plans perceived within the mind; sura-isvaraih--by the controllers of the universe like Brahma and Siva; sahasankarsanah-- along with Sankarsana (Baladeva); cakre--performed; bhagavan--the Supreme Personality of Godhead; madhu-sudanah--the killer of the Madhu demon.

Translation

The Supreme Personality of Godhead, Krsna, with the cooperation of Sankarsana, Balarama, performed activities beyond the mental comprehension of even such personalities as Lord Brahma and Lord Siva. [For instance, Krsna arranged the Battle of Kuruksetra to kill many demons for the relief of the entire world.]
