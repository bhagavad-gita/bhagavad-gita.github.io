9.6.44

Text

tasam kalir abhud bhuyams tad-arthe 'pohya sauhrdam mamanurupo nayam va iti tad-gata-cetasam

Synonyms

tasam--of all the princesses; kalih--disagreement and quarrel; abhut--there was; bhuyan--very much; tatarthe-- for the sake of Saubhari Muni; apohya--giving up; sauhrdam--a good relationship; mama--mine; anurupah--the fit person; na--not; ayam--this; vah--yours; iti--in this way; tat-gata-cetasam--being attracted by him.

Translation

Thereafter, the princesses, being attracted by Saubhari Muni, gave up their sisterly relationship and quarreled among themselves, each one of them contending, "This man is just suitable for me, and not for you." In this way there ensued a great disagreement.
