9.23.50

Text

viprstho dhrtadevayam eka anakadundubheh santidevatmaja rajan prasama-prasitadayah

Synonyms

viprsthah--Viprstha; dhrtadevayam--in the womb of the wife named Dhrtadeva; ekah--one son; anakadundubheh--of Anakadundubhi, Vasudeva; santideva-atmajah--the sons of another wife, named Santideva; rajan--O Maharaja Pariksit; prasama-prasita-adayah--Prasama, Prasita and other sons.

Translation

From the womb of Dhrtadeva, one of the wives of Anakadundubhi [Vasudeva], came a son named Viprstha. The sons of Santideva, another wife of Vasudeva, were Prasama, Prasita and others.
