9.4.11

Text

grhana dravinam dattam mat-satra-parisesitam ity uktvantarhito rudro bhagavan dharma-vatsalah

Synonyms

grhana--please take now; dravinam--all the wealth; dattam--is given (to you by me); mat-satraparisesitam-- the remnants of the sacrifice executed on my behalf; iti uktva--after speaking like this; antarhitah--disappeared; rudrah--Lord Siva; bhagavan--the most powerful demigod; dharma-vatsalah-- adherent to the principles of religion.

Translation

Lord Siva said, "Now you may take all the wealth remaining from the sacrifice, for I give it to you." After saying this, Lord Siva, who is most adherent to the religious principles, disappeared from that place.
