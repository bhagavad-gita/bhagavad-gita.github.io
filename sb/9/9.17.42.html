9.17.42

Text

aprcchat tanayam purum vayasonam gunadhikam na tvam agrajavad vatsa mam pratyakhyatum arhasi

Synonyms

aprcchat--requested; tanayam--the son; purum--Puru; vayasa--by age; unam--although younger; gunaadhikam-- better than the others by quality; na--not; tvam--you; agraja-vat--like your older brothers; vatsa--my dear son; mam--me; pratyakhyatum--to refuse; arhasi--ought.

Translation

King Yayati then requested Puru, who was younger than these three brothers but more qualified, "My dear son, do not be disobedient like your elder brothers, for that is not your duty."
