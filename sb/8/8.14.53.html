8.14.53

Text

acaryam jnana-sampannam vastrabharana-dhenubhih tosayed rtvijas caiva tad viddhy aradhanam hareh

Synonyms

acaryam--the spiritual master; jnana-sampannam--very advanced in spiritual knowledge; vastraabharana- dhenubhih--with clothing, ornaments and many cows; tosayet--should satisfy; rtvijah--the priests recommended by the spiritual master; ca eva--as well as; tat viddhi--try to understand that; aradhanam--worship; hareh--of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

Translation

One should satisfy the spiritual master [acarya], who is very learned in Vedic literature, and should satisfy his assistant priests [known as hota, udgata, adhvaryu and brahma]. One should please them by offering them clothing, ornaments and cows. This is the ceremony called visnu-aradhana, or worship of Lord Visnu.
