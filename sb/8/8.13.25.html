8.13.25

Text

bhagavann udyamo bhuyan baler nah purva-vairinah avisahyam imam manye kenasit tejasorjitah

Synonyms

bhagavan--O my lord; udyamah--enthusiasm; bhuyan--great; baleh--of Bali Maharaja; nah--our; purvavairinah-- past enemy; avisahyam--unbearable; imam--this; manye--I think; kena--by whom; asit--got; tejasa--prowess; urjitah--achieved.

Translation

My lord, our old enemy Bali Maharaja now has new enthusiasm, and he has obtained such astonishing power that we think that perhaps we cannot resist his prowess.
