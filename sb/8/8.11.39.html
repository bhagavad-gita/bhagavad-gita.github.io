11.39

39

Text

tam daivim giram akarnya maghavan susamahitah dhyayan phenam athapasyad upayam ubhayatmakam

Synonyms

tam--that; daivim--ominous; giram--voice; akarnya--after hearing; maghavan--Lord Indra; susamahitah-- becoming very careful; dhyayan--meditating; phenam--appearance of foam; atha--thereafter; apasyat--he saw; upayam--the means; ubhaya-atmakam--simultaneously dry and moist.

Translation

After hearing the ominous voice, Indra, with great attention, began to meditate on how to kill the demon. He then saw that foam would be the means, for it is neither moist nor dry.
