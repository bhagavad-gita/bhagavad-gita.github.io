8.14.46

Text

payo-bhakso vratam idam cared visnv-arcanadrtah purvavaj juhuyad agnim brahmanams capi bhojayet

Synonyms

payah-bhaksah--one who drinks milk only; vratam idam--this process of worshiping with a vow; caret-- one should execute; visnu-arcana-adrtah--worshiping Lord Visnu with great faith and devotion; purvavat-- as prescribed previously; juhuyat--one should offer oblations; agnim--into the fire; brahmanan--unto the brahmanas; ca api--as well as; bhojayet--should feed.

Translation

Worshiping Lord Visnu with great faith and devotion and living only by drinking milk, one should follow this vow. One should also offer oblations to the fire and feed the brahmanas as mentioned before.
