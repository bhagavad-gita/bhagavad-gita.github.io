12 agnayo 'tithayo bhrtya bhiksavo ye ca lipsavah sarvam bhagavato brahmann anudhyanan na risyati SYNONYMS agnayah--worshiping the fires; atithayah--receiving the guests; bhrtyah--satisfying the servants; bhiksavah--pleasing the beggars; ye--all of them who; ca--and; lipsavah--as they desire (are taken care of); sarvam--all of them; bhagavatah--of you, my lord; brahman--O brahmana; anudhyanat--from always thinking; na risyati--nothing is missed (everything is properly done). TRANSLATION O beloved husband, the fires, guests, servants and beggars are all being properly cared for by me. Because I always think of you, there is no possibility that any of the religious principles will be neglected.
