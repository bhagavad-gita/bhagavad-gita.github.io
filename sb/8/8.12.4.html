8.12.4

Text

aditya vasavo rudra visvedeva marud-ganah asvinav rbhavo rajann indras tesam purandarah

Synonyms

adityah--the Adityas; vasavah--the Vasus; rudrah--the Rudras; visvedevah--the Visvedevas; marutganah-- and the Maruts; asvinau--the two Asvini brothers; rbhavah--the Rbhus; rajan--O King; indrah--the king of heaven; tesam--of them; purandarah--Purandara.

Translation

In this manvantara, O King, the Adityas, the Vasus, the Rudras, the Visvedevas, the Maruts, the two Asvini-kumara brothers and the Rbhus are the demigods. Their head king [Indra] is Purandara.
