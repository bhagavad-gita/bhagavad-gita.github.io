8.14.43

Text

krtva sirasi tac-chesam devam udvasayet tatah dvy-avaran bhojayed vipran payasena yathocitam

Synonyms

krtva--taking; sirasi--on the head; tat-sesam--all the remnants (the water and flowers offered to the Deity); devam--unto the Deity; udvasayet--should be thrown into a sacred place; tatah--thereafter; dviavaran-- a minimum of two; bhojayet--should feed; vipran--brahmanas; payasena--with sweet rice; yathaucitam-- as each deserves.

Translation

After touching to one's head all the flowers and water offered to the Deity, one should throw them into a sacred place. Then one should feed at least two brahmanas with sweet rice.
