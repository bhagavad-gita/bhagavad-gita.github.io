22.56

56

Text

asrausid rsibhih sakam atma-tattvam asamsayam navy asino bhagavata proktam brahma sanatanam

Synonyms

asrausit--he heard; rsibhih--the great saintly persons; sakam--with; atma-tattvam--the science of selfrealization; asamsayam--without any doubt (because it was spoken by the Supreme Lord); navi asinah-- sitting in the boat; bhagavata--by the Supreme Personality of Godhead; proktam--explained; brahma--all transcendental literatures; sanatanam--eternally existing.

Translation

While sitting in the boat, King Satyavrata, accompanied by the great saintly persons, listened to the instructions of the Supreme Personality of Godhead in regard to self-realization. These instructions were all from the eternal Vedic literature [brahma]. Thus the King and sages had no doubt about the Absolute Truth.
