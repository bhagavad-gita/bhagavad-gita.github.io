8.22.44

Text

so 'nudhyatas tato rajna pradurasin maharnave eka-srnga-dharo matsyo haimo niyuta-yojanah

Synonyms

sah--the Lord; anudhyatah--being meditated upon; tatah--thereafter (hearing the words of the saintly brahmanas); rajna--by the King; pradurasit--appeared (before him); maha-arnave--in the great ocean of inundation; eka-srnga-dharah--with one horn; matsyah--a big fish; haimah--made of gold; niyutayojanah-- eight million miles long.

Translation

Then, while the King constantly meditated upon the Supreme Personality of Godhead, a large golden fish appeared in the ocean of inundation. The fish had one horn and was eight million miles long.
