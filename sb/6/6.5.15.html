6.5.15

Text

vastor angirasi-putro visvakarmakrti-patih tato manus caksuso 'bhud visve sadhya manoh sutah

Synonyms

vastoh--of Vastu; angirasi--of his wife named Angirasi; putrah--the son; visvakarma--Visvakarma; akrtipatih-- the husband of Akrti; tatah--from them; manuh caksusah--the Manu named Caksusa; abhut--was born; visve--the Visvadevas; sadhyah--the Sadhyas; manoh--of Manu; sutah--the sons.

Translation

From Angirasi, the wife of the Vasu named Vastu, was born the great architect Visvakarma. Visvakarma became the husband of Akrti, from whom the Manu named Caksusa was born. The sons of Manu were known as the Visvadevas and Sadhyas.
