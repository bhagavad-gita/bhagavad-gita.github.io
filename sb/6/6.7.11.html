7.11

11

Text

aho bata mayasadhu krtam vai dabhra-buddhina yan mayaisvarya-mattena guruh sadasi katkrtah

Synonyms

aho--alas; bata--indeed; maya--by me; asadhu--disrespectful; krtam--the action done; vai--certainly; dabhra-buddhina--being of less intelligence; yat--because; maya--by me; aisvarya-mattena--being very proud of material opulence; guruh--the spiritual master; sadasi--in this assembly; kat-krtah--mistreated.

Translation

Alas, what a regrettable deed I have committed because of my lack of intelligence and my pride in my material opulences. I failed to show respect to my spiritual master when he entered this assembly, and thus I have insulted him.
