6.10.24

Text

avidhya parigham vrtrah karsnayasam arindamah indraya prahinod ghoram vama-hastena marisa

Synonyms

avidhya--whirling; parigham--the club; vrtrah--Vrtrasura; karsna-ayasam--made of iron; arim-damah-- who was competent to subdue his enemy; indraya--at Indra; prahinot--threw; ghoram--very fearful; vamahastena-- with his left hand; marisa--O best of kings, Maharaja Pariksit.

Translation

O Maharaja Pariksit, Vrtrasura, who was completely able to subdue his enemy, took his iron club, whirled it around, aimed it at Indra and then threw it at him with his left hand.
