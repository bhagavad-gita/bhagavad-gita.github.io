6.6.3

Text

sri-badarayanir uvaca vrtah purohitas tvastro mahendrayanuprcchate narayanakhyam varmaha tad ihaika-manah srnu

Synonyms

sri-badarayanih uvaca--Sri Sukadeva Gosvami said; vrtah--the chosen; purohitah--priest; tvastrah--the son of Tvasta; mahendraya--unto King Indra; anuprcchate--after he (Indra) inquired; narayana-akhyam-- named Narayana-kavaca; varma--defensive armor made of a mantra; aha--he said; tat--that; iha--this; ekamanah-- with great attention; srnu--hear from me.

Translation

Sri Sukadeva Gosvami said: King Indra, the leader of the demigods, inquired about the armor known as Narayana-kavaca from Visvarupa, who was engaged by the demigods as their priest. Please hear Visvarupa's reply with great attention.
