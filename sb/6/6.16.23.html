6.16.23

Text

sri-suka uvaca hata-putra ditih sakraparsni- grahena visnuna manyuna soka-diptena jvalanti paryacintayat

Synonyms

sri-sukah uvaca--Sri Sukadeva Gosvami said; hata-putra--whose sons were killed; ditih--Diti; sakraparsni- grahena--who was helping Lord Indra; visnuna--by Lord Visnu; manyuna--with anger; sokadiptena-- kindled by lamentation; jvalanti--burning; paryacintayat--thought.

Translation

Sri Sukadeva Gosvami said: Just to help Indra, Lord Visnu killed the two brothers Hiranyaksa and Hiranyakasipu. Because of their being killed, their mother, Diti, overwhelmed with lamentation and anger, contemplated as follows.
